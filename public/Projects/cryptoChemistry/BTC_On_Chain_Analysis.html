Total amount of coins transferred on-chain
Bitcoin transactions involve the transfer of a specified amount of Bitcoin from one address (sender's wallet) to another address (recipient's wallet).
These transactions are recorded and verified by network participants known as miners, who add the transaction details to blocks on the blockchain.
Total amount of fees in USD paid to miners
The total amount of fees in USD paid to miners for Bitcoin refers to the cumulative sum of transaction fees that have been paid by users for including their transactions in the Bitcoin blockchain.
These fees are collected by miners as a reward for including and validating transactions in a new block.
The peaks in the total amount of fees paid to miners in USD in 2017 and 2021 signify the tops in the bitcoin market cycle,
while in 2023, the peak is expected to be attributed to the launch of ordinal-based bitcoin smart contracts.
