Total Crypto Market Cap
The total crypto market cap refers to the combined value of all cryptocurrencies in existence.
It represents the overall market valuation of the entire cryptocurrency industry at any given point in time.
The market cap is calculated by multiplying the circulating supply of a cryptocurrency by its current market price.

Total Crypto Market Cap Excluding Bitcoin
The total crypto market capitalization excluding Bitcoin refers to the combined value of all cryptocurrencies in existence, excluding the market value of Bitcoin.
Total Crypto Market Cap Excluding Bitcoin and Ethereum
The total crypto market capitalization excluding Bitcoin and excluding Ethereum refers to the combined value of all cryptocurrencies in existence, excluding the market value of Bitcoin and market value of Ethereum.
