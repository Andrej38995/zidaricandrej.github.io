Bitcoin Price ($)
The graph illustrating the fluctuations in the value of Bitcoin against the US dollar over a 12-month period serves as a crucial tool for traders and investors.
Bitcoin, being a decentralized digital currency, experiences significant price movements, making it imperative for market participants to closely monitor these changes.
By studying this chart, traders and investors can gain insights into the historical performance of Bitcoin, identify patterns, and detect potential trends.

The chart displays a bull market support band, consisting of two moving averages.
The first moving average is represented by a red line, corresponding to the 20-week simple moving average (equivalent to a 140-day moving average on a daily interval).
The second moving average is depicted by a green line, specifically the 21-week exponential moving average (equivalent to a 147-day moving average on a daily interval).

Within a bull market, this support band plays a critical role by offering significant price support.
When the price re-tests and successfully holds, it indicates a solid foundation for future price movements.
Conversely, during a bear market, this band functions as a strong resistance level and acts as a bounce point for further downward movement.
